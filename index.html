What is Game of Thrones?

Game of Thrones is an HBO series based on the George R.R. Martin novels Song of Ice and Fire. The series has received 212 awards and 611 nominations as of 2017. The story is set in a Westeros and Essos, two fictional continents. There are many characters and the plot follows the succession of power over the course of years; A Game of Thrones.

The series adaptation was create by David Benioff and D.B. Weiss and is shot all over the world, including Canada, the United Kingdom, Icelean, Morocco, and Malta. The series has set records for HBO viewership and is critically acclaimed for it's complex storyline, quality acting, costumes, cinematography, and production.
